How It Works

1. Configure your OpenRouter API key and preferred model in Settings.
2. Paste an Instagram Reel, TikTok, or YouTube link on the Import tab.
3. The AI generates a structured workout with exercises grouped by muscle group — chest, back, legs, etc.
4. Edit sets, reps and weights inline, then save.
5. All workouts are saved to your device and viewable in History.

Data & Backup

Your workouts are saved locally on this device. Export a backup or import from another device.
